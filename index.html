ABOUT ME

  • I am Yuan Chen, a current Ph.D. student of Computational Mathematics in Ohio State University.
  • My research interests fall in the broad areas of scientific computing and numerical analysis. I develop tools to solve Partial Differential Equations (PDE) and related models arising in applications. Currently I am working on Discontinuous Galerkin Method (DG) with Dr. Yulong Xing and Immersed Finite Element Method (IFEM) with Dr. Xu Zhang and Dr. Songming Hou.
  • Please see Research and CV for more information about my research, if you are interested in these topics, feel free to contact me to have a discussion!
